VR Delivery Plan

Week 3, Day 3 · ✍️ Written Response

Points: 20

🔗 Connection to Lesson: This plan is your production document for VR recording.

📋 Assignment

Plan your VR delivery:

1. VOCAL SCORE: Describe your plan for the three most important moments (hook, climax, closing). Note pace, volume, pause choices.
2. MNEMONIC AIDS: What will you use? How will they help without becoming a crutch?
3. PROPS/MULTIMEDIA: Visual aids? What and how do they support your argument?

📤 How to Submit

Answer all three sections in the text box or upload a document. Be specific.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • Vocal score covers three key moments with specific choices
  • Mnemonic aids described with clear strategy
  • Props/multimedia decision explained with reasoning
  • Plan is specific enough to follow during recording
⚠️ Heads Up
Read your response aloud before submitting. If it doesn't make sense spoken, revise it. Your teacher is looking for genuine thinking, not filler. Short and honest beats long and vague.
