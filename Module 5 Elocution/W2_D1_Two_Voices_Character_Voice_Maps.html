Two Voices - Character Voice Maps

Project: American Voices in Dialogue
Objective: Create voice profiles for solo multi-voice performance.
Standards: ELA.11.R.1.2, ELA.11.C.2.1

Lesson Content

Perform two voices solo. Choose: Whitman/Dickinson, Douglass/Hughes, or Stanton/Millay. Build a Voice Map for each.

Activities

Multiple Choice

Douglass and Hughes profiles?

A. Both loud
B. Douglass: booming, confrontational. Hughes: quiet, confident.
C. Douglass: whispering. Hughes: screaming.
D. Identical tone.
Contrast is the power.
Written Response

Voice Map for EACH: era, belief, emotion, vocal qualities, physical energy.
