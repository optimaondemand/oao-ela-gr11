Oration Draft

Week 3, Day 2 · ✍️ Written Response

Points: 30

🔗 Connection to Lesson: This draft is the text you will perform in VR. It needs to sound like speech, not like a paper read aloud.

📋 Assignment

Submit your complete oration (600-750 words, approximately 4-5 minutes). This should be a NEW document adapted from your Module 4 essay:

- Spoken opening hook
- Thesis as a clear spoken claim
- Strongest arguments with evidence woven naturally
- Counterclaim acknowledged and rebutted
- Closing that resonates

📤 How to Submit

Upload a document or paste your full oration into the text box.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • Draft is 600-750 words and adapted for oral delivery
  • Opening hook grabs attention; thesis is speakable
  • Evidence is woven naturally without academic citation style
  • Counterclaim is present and rebutted
  • Closing moves the listener
⚠️ Heads Up
Read your response aloud before submitting. If it doesn't make sense spoken, revise it. Your teacher is looking for genuine thinking, not filler. Short and honest beats long and vague.
