Pace Pitch and Pause

Project: Revolutionary Voice
Objective: Experiment with pacing, pitch, and strategic pauses.
Standards: ELA.11.C.2.1, ELA.K12.EE.6.1

Lesson Content

Pace: Speed creates urgency; slowing signals importance.

Pitch: Variation engages listeners. Rising = questions; falling = authority.

Pause: A well-placed pause gives phrases weight.

Patrick Henry built to "Give me liberty, or give me death!" with deliberate pacing.
