Unit Reflection

Week 3, Day 5 · ✍️ Written Response

Points: 25

🔗 Connection to Lesson: This reflection asks you to step back and articulate what you learned about speaking, about yourself, and about how your voice carries meaning.

📋 Assignment

Answer ALL (aim for a full page):

1. Which project felt most authentic? Why?
2. What vocal technique had the biggest impact? Specific example.
3. How was the oration different from the essay? What did you discover?
4. How did self-recording change your understanding?
5. What is elocution? How will you apply these skills?

📤 How to Submit

Type your full reflection or upload a document. Aim for at least one full page.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • All five questions answered thoughtfully
  • Specific examples from recordings referenced
  • Genuine self-awareness and growth shown
  • Future application is concrete
  • Writing is clear, honest, and substantive
⚠️ Heads Up
Read your response aloud before submitting. If it doesn't make sense spoken, revise it. Your teacher is looking for genuine thinking, not filler. Short and honest beats long and vague.
