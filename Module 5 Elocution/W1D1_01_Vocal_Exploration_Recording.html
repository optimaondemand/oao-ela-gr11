Vocal Exploration Recording

Week 1, Day 1 · 🎤 Performance Recording

Points: 10

🔗 Connection to Lesson: This activity connects to today's lesson on diaphragmatic breathing and projection. You are experimenting with how the same words change meaning when delivered differently.

📋 Assignment

Find a quiet space and read this line THREE different ways (urgent/angry, calm/logical, inspiring/hopeful):

"We hold these truths to be self-evident, that all men are created equal."

Record all three back-to-back in a single recording.

📤 How to Submit

Upload your audio or video recording. In the text submission box, describe what you changed vocally for each version (2-3 sentences per version).

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • All three versions are clearly distinct in tone, pace, or pitch
  • Text submission describes specific vocal changes for each version
  • Recording is audible and complete
⚠️ Heads Up
Make sure your recording is audible and complete before submitting. Record in a quiet space. If using video, ensure your face and upper body are visible. You may re-record and resubmit before the due date.
