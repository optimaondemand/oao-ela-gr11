🔗 Connection to Lesson: This is the culmination of the entire Elocution Unit.

📋 Assignment

Record and submit your final 4-5 minute oration in VR. Deliver your adapted Module 4 argument with conviction, use your vocal score, employ mnemonic aids naturally, incorporate props/multimedia with intention, and end strong.

This is YOUR voice joining the American conversation.

📤 How to Submit

Upload your final VR recording. This is your polished, best take for Project 3 and the culminating assessment of the Elocution Unit.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • Performance is 4-5 minutes, complete
  • Argument is clear, logical, and persuasive on first listen
  • Vocal delivery demonstrates mastery of pace, pitch, pause, emphasis
  • Speaker is authentic and invested
  • Mnemonic aids used smoothly
  • Props/multimedia enhance rather than distract
  • Opening hooks; closing resonates
  • Performance reflects growth across the unit
⚠️ Heads Up
Make sure your recording is audible and complete before submitting. Record in a quiet space. If using video, ensure your face and upper body are visible. You may re-record and resubmit before the due date.
