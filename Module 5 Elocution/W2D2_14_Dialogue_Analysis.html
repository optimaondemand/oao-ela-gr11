Dialogue Analysis

Week 2, Day 2 · ✍️ Written Response

Points: 15

🔗 Connection to Lesson: Understanding the conversation between your texts elevates your performance from two readings to a dialogue.

📋 Assignment

Explain the conversation your arrangement creates. What question or theme connects the two voices? How do they agree, clash, or build on each other? What does a listener understand by hearing them together that they would not get from either text alone?

📤 How to Submit

Write a thoughtful paragraph in the text box.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • Identifies a connecting theme or question
  • Explains the relationship between voices
  • Articulates what the dialogue reveals that solo reading does not
⚠️ Heads Up
Read your response aloud before submitting. If it doesn't make sense spoken, revise it. Your teacher is looking for genuine thinking, not filler. Short and honest beats long and vague.
