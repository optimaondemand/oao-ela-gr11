Practice Performance Recording

Week 1, Day 4 · 🎤 Performance Recording

Points: 15

🔗 Connection to Lesson: This practice recording gives you material to self-evaluate and gives your teacher a chance to offer feedback.

📋 Assignment

Record your full 2-3 minute practice performance of your annotated passage from Day 3. Deliver it as if this were your final take.

📤 How to Submit

Upload your audio or video recording. This is a practice take; your teacher will provide feedback to help you improve for your final performance tomorrow.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • Recording is 2-3 minutes and covers the full annotated passage
  • Delivery shows evidence of annotation choices
  • Speaker demonstrates investment and effort
⚠️ Heads Up
Make sure your recording is audible and complete before submitting. Record in a quiet space. If using video, ensure your face and upper body are visible. You may re-record and resubmit before the due date.
