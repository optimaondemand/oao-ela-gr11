Revision Recording

Week 2, Day 4 · 🎤 Performance Recording

Points: 10

🔗 Connection to Lesson: Targeted revision mirrors how professionals work: identify, fix, test, evaluate.

📋 Assignment

Record 60-90 seconds of your weakest section with your adjustments applied.

📤 How to Submit

Upload your recording. In the text box, compare this version to yesterday's and describe whether the adjustment worked.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • Recording targets the weakest section
  • Adjustments are audible
  • Text reflects on whether changes improved performance
⚠️ Heads Up
Make sure your recording is audible and complete before submitting. Record in a quiet space. If using video, ensure your face and upper body are visible. You may re-record and resubmit before the due date.
