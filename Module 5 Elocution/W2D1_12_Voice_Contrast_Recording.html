Voice Contrast Recording

Week 2, Day 1 · 🎤 Performance Recording

Points: 15

🔗 Connection to Lesson: This recording tests whether your Voice Maps translate into actual vocal performance.

📋 Assignment

Record one short excerpt (2-3 sentences) as Speaker A, then another as Speaker B. Focus on making the two voices as vocally distinct as possible.

📤 How to Submit

Upload your recording. In the text box, describe what you changed between voices.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • Both speakers are clearly distinguishable
  • Text submission identifies specific vocal and physical changes
  • Choices align with the Character Voice Maps
⚠️ Heads Up
Make sure your recording is audible and complete before submitting. Record in a quiet space. If using video, ensure your face and upper body are visible. You may re-record and resubmit before the due date.
