VR Practice Recording

Week 3, Day 4 · 🎤 Performance Recording

Points: 20

🔗 Connection to Lesson: This practice recording is your last chance to identify what needs to change.

📋 Assignment

Record your complete 4-5 minute practice oration in VR. Full conviction, vocal score applied, mnemonic aids used naturally, props/multimedia timed.

📤 How to Submit

Upload your VR recording. This is your dress rehearsal.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • Recording is 4-5 minutes covering the full oration
  • Vocal delivery shows intentional choices
  • Mnemonic aids used without becoming a crutch
  • Props/multimedia timed and purposeful
  • Speaker demonstrates conviction
⚠️ Heads Up
Make sure your recording is audible and complete before submitting. Record in a quiet space. If using video, ensure your face and upper body are visible. You may re-record and resubmit before the due date.
