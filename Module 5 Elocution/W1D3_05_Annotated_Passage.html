Annotated Passage

Week 1, Day 3 · ✍️ Written Response

Points: 20

🔗 Connection to Lesson: This annotated passage becomes your performance blueprint for the rest of the week.

📋 Assignment

Choose a 4-6 sentence passage from Patrick Henry, Thomas Paine, or one of the Federalist Papers. Write it out with performance annotations:

// = short pause, //// = long pause, CAPS = emphasis, [tone] notes, arrows = pitch, (blocking) = physical movement.

Then explain your choices in 2-3 sentences.

📤 How to Submit

Submit a document or type directly into the text box. Your annotated passage and explanation should be clearly formatted.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • Passage is 4-6 sentences from an approved text
  • Annotations use the system taught in the lesson
  • 2-3 sentence explanation connects annotation choices to rhetorical intent
⚠️ Heads Up
Read your response aloud before submitting. If it doesn't make sense spoken, revise it. Your teacher is looking for genuine thinking, not filler. Short and honest beats long and vague.
