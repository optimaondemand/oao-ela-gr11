Self-Evaluation

Week 1, Day 4 · ✍️ Written Response

Points: 15

🔗 Connection to Lesson: Self-evaluation is the core skill of this lesson. Your honesty here directly improves your final performance.

📋 Assignment

After listening to your practice recording, rate yourself (Strong / Developing / Needs Work) on each criterion and explain:

1. Projection & Clarity
2. Pace & Pause
3. Tone & Emotion
4. Emphasis on Key Words
5. Overall Conviction

Then identify the ONE adjustment that would most improve your delivery.

📤 How to Submit

Type your ratings and explanations directly in the text box. Graded on thoughtfulness, not on how well the performance went.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • All five criteria rated and explained with specific evidence
  • Ratings are honest, not inflated
  • One clear, specific adjustment identified
⚠️ Heads Up
Read your response aloud before submitting. If it doesn't make sense spoken, revise it. Your teacher is looking for genuine thinking, not filler. Short and honest beats long and vague.
