Rewriting for the Ear

Project: Personal Oration
Objective: Write 600-750 word oration draft.
Standards: ELA.11.C.2.1, ELA.11.C.1.5, ELA.K12.EE.5.1

Lesson Content

Shorter sentences. Repetition for power. Natural citations. Spoken transitions. Keep counterclaims. Close by moving the listener.

Activities

Written Response

Submit complete oration (600-750 words). Spoken hook, thesis, arguments, counterclaim, resonant closing. NEW document.

Multiple Choice

Adapting a long academic sentence about Hamilton and judicial independence?

A. Read as written
B. Cut entirely
C. Break into short punchy statements
D. "Hamilton liked judges."
Keep content, break into statements listeners can process one at a time.
Written Response

Annotate: ethos/pathos/logos, repetition, peak intensity, and resistance points.
