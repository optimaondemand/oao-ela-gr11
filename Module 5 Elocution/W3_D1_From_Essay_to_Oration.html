From Essay to Oration

Project: Personal Oration
Objective: Begin adapting Module 4 essay into 4-5 minute VR oration.
Standards: ELA.11.C.2.1, ELA.11.C.1.5, ELA.K12.EE.6.1

Lesson Content

Transform your Module 4 essay into a VR oration. What stays: thesis, argument, evidence. What changes: shorter sentences, spoken signposts, opening hook. What's added: personal conviction.

Activities

Multiple Choice

Essay opens with academic statement about factions. Stronger hook?

A. Keep as-is
B. "I will discuss factions."
C. "240 years ago, Madison warned factions would tear us apart. Open any news app today."
D. "Factions are important."
Link historical warning to everyday experience for tension.
Written Response

Adaptation outline: Opening hook, thesis as spoken claim, 2-3 key points (how to present orally), new closing.

Written Response

Performance Intent: What should listeners think/feel/do? Where will conviction be strongest?
