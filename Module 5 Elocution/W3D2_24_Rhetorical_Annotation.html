Rhetorical Annotation

Week 3, Day 2 · ✍️ Written Response

Points: 15

🔗 Connection to Lesson: This annotation connects your rhetorical knowledge from Module 4 to your performance preparation.

📋 Assignment

Annotate your oration draft:

1. Where you use ethos, pathos, and logos (label at least one of each)
2. Where you use repetition or parallel structure
3. The moment of peak emotional intensity
4. One place where a listener might resist, and how your delivery handles it

📤 How to Submit

Submit an annotated version or type annotations in the text box with clear references.

🎯 What Your Teacher Is Looking For

  • At least one instance each of ethos, pathos, logos identified
  • Repetition or parallel structure noted
  • Peak intensity moment identified
  • Resistance point identified with delivery strategy
⚠️ Heads Up
Read your response aloud before submitting. If it doesn't make sense spoken, revise it. Your teacher is looking for genuine thinking, not filler. Short and honest beats long and vague.
