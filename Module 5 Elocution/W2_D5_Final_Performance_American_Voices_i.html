Week 2, Day 5: Final Performance: American Voices in Dialogue

Project: American Voices in Dialogue

Objective: Submit your polished 3-4 minute solo multi-voice performance.

Standards: ELA.11.R.1.2, ELA.11.C.2.1, ELA.K12.EE.6.1

💡 Key Concept: When two voices interact, the listener hears connections and tensions that are invisible in either text alone. That is the power of dialogue.

📖 Lesson

Final recording for Project 2. You have built distinct voices, structured a dialogue, rehearsed, and refined.

  • Ground yourself. Settle into your first character.
  • Commit fully to each voice. Don't let them drift toward each other.
  • Trust your transitions. Pause, shift, launch with conviction.
  • Your final line should land. Hold the silence after.
  • This is a conversation, not two readings back to back.
✅ Pro Tip
Record in a quiet space with good lighting. Make sure your face and upper body are visible so your physical shifts between voices are clear.
