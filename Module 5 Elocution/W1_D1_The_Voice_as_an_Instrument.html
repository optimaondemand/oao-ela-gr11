The Voice as an Instrument

Project: Revolutionary Voice
Objective: Understand diaphragmatic breathing and projection.
Standards: ELA.11.C.2.1, ELA.K12.EE.6.1

Lesson Content

Welcome to the Elocution Unit! Over the next three weeks, you will develop your skills as a speaker and performer through independent projects.

Great speakers don't just say words; they deliver them. Projection is about supporting your voice with breath from your diaphragm.

Today you'll experiment with how breath control changes power and clarity. Find a quiet space to speak aloud.
